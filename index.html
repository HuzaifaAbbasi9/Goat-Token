Our Features

BuyBack

when the buyback function is turned on, tokens are bought back from the market, resulting in an immediate on the price

Burn

the tokens bought through buyback are immediately burned. this creates a true burn and guarantees the price per token will increase every time a buy back is activated

rewards

holders are additionally ''auto-staked '' instantly receiving 8% of the transaction volume and they can watch their wallet grow in real-time

Anti-Dump Lock & 3% Sell Fee

Sells are restricted to less than 0.1% of the total supply + extra 3% fee is applied to all sells. This will allow us to reduce swing-trading and break whales control.

Secured and Safe

Initial Liquidity Provided will be locked with DXSALE for 1 years.
